# Pecto Beta 1.1 Patch Notes

---

## Overview

After the Pecto pounding, some core issues with the game have come to light, most of which are card design issues. This update serves more as a shift in philosophy for balance as opposed to a full gameplay overall (although almost every card will be getting a tweak).

Here is a list of things that are being tweaked/removed this update:

- Card playability
- Destruction
- High access to recursion
- More reward for higher LVL cards
- Free repeatable abilities
- Going second

---

## System Updates

### Game Board

- Back row zones increased from 2 -> 3

This change makes it so now every front row card has a specific card to attack instead of having to choose while in the center row. It also allows players to play more cards overall, which has been a point of contention since moving to 5 zones.

### Card Draw

- Card Draw has been simplified to be draw 2 per turn.
- You no longer recycle your deck if your deck is empty. Instead, if you were to draw cards from your deck and its empty you take that much damage. So that means at the start of your turn if your deck is empty and you were to draw 2, you take 2 damage.

### Going First vs Second

- The player going first does not draw on their first turn
- Players can now attack on their first turn, but cannot target players for attacks

### Bleed Keyword

- Bleed counters are now removed when healing
- 1 Bleed counter is removed for each point healed
- This also applies while at full health

### Effect Keyword

- Removed.

Effect has proven to be largely useless as well as quite confusing so for now it is being removed entirely. Due to this, some cards are going to be replaced outright since they only function as effect cards.

### Response Keyword

- Removed.

This purely refers to cards that could be set from the hand as a response. The response keyword while not really problematic, did go against one of the core philosophies of the game which was having all interaction being visible and on the board at all times. The response keyword also added onto some game state deadlock.

### Muted Keyword

- Cards with muted are now unable to target the player for attacks.

### Perish

- A card now perishes when it loses all of its FORCE.

### Sacrfice

- When a card is sacrificed, its FORCE is now immediately set to 0.

---

## Balance Philosophies

### Destruction

Destruction has been proven to be an extremely difficult thing to balance and Pecto and is almost always problematic. Due to this, destruction is being removed from the game in favour of direct damage instead.

Direct damage is a much healthier system in Pecto since stat buffing cards is the intended way for it to be played.

This also removes a piece of rules complexity/annoyance related to destruction.

Along with this change, if a card takes excess damage, the excess damage effectively does not exist. For example, Voodoo Demon says "When Voodoo Demon takes damage, add that many bleed counters to any target.". Voodoo Demon has 3 Force. If you dealt 5 damage to Voodoo Demon, it would only take 3 damage and add 3 bleed counters.

### Recursion

Recursion is a very strong mechanic in Pecto, but is also one of the more fun/interesting things about the game. Due to this, recursion is not being removed but it is being toned down substantially.

Things that recycle your deck are being untouched, as that is completely fine.

Cards that bring things back from your discard in anyway must have some cost beyond when you get the cards, whether that be discarding cards or losing life.

### Life as a Resource

Life costs are being increased overall this patch, as 10 life is difficult to break through sometimes.

### LVL 5 Cards

I have traditionally been scared to make LVL 5 cards strong as I didn't feel they were as meaningful as a 5 cost card in Magic or Hearthstone. While this is true, LVL 5 cards across the board (as well as LVL 4) are quite weak and they are all largely being buffed this patch.

### Muted, Static, Delayed

Starting this patch I will be much more sparing with using these keywords, especially static.

### Stats

Due to the direct damage change listed above, stats are being buffed overall. Here is the new standard stat baseline across the board:

- LVL 1: 1-2 FORCE
- LVL 2: 2-4 FORCE
- LVL 3: 3-5 FORCE
- LVL 4: 4-6 FORCE
- LVL 5: 5+ FORCE

Some cards will still remain understated depending on their effects, but largely cards with vastly understated FORCE are being removed. 0 FORCE cards in particular are going to be toned down so they can have more utility out of just activating them.

Cards that have strong scaling effects like Last Mass, Black Hole, Serendipity will be generally understated since they have large potential upsides.

### Tutoring

Tutoring is being toned down slightly, mainly the Magic Link mechanic. While tutoring isn't super problematic to the game, it is a pinch to strong currently. I will be hitting tutoring less overall since I believe mass recursion is the larger issue at play.
