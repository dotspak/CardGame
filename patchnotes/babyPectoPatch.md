# Baby Pecto Ruleset

## Draw System

- Draw up to 4 each turn.

## Card LVL

- Cards can only be from LVL 1-3.

## Zones

- 6 Zones, 3 Front, 3 Back.

## Defending

- When defending, when the opponent attacks an open zone, if you control a back row card in the same column as the card attacking, you may choose to block it with your back row card. Otherwise, you take player damage.

## Going 2nd

- The player going second can attack, but cannot declare attacks on the player.

## New Keyword: Spell

- Cards with the spell keyword can be cast immediately upon being set (opponent can't respond to it being set + cast) and can be set as a response.

## Bleed

- Bleed counters are now removed upon taking bleed damage.

## Mulligans

- You may only replace cards from your opening hand of 4.
- You receive new cards after choosing all cards to replace.
