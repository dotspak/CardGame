# Pecto Set 2 Ideas

## New Card Archetypes

### Frog Archetype

Frogs will be based around 2 things:

- Fragile keyword
- Moving cards around

### Fish Archetype

Fish will be a defensive archetype

- Life gain
- Large force gain based on fish count

## New Mechanics

### Slaughter Trigger

- Triggers when the opponent controls no cards.
- Triggers the instant this occurs as well as if a card with a slaughter trigger is summoned and the opponent controls no cards.
