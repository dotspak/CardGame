# :star:Pecto V5.2 Patch Notes - The Magical Update:star:

---

## Overview

This patch aims to rework the spells mechanic a bit further making them more draft friendly, as well as more interesting/powerful in general. Many spell cards inate effects are being buffed, but the "spell on board" mechanic from the previous patch is being improved further.

---

## Ruleset Updates

### Damage Phase

I've decided to scrap the damage phase. It was a fun mechanic in testing but I realized after more thought it solves an issue that doesn't really exist, and I'm in favour of less mechanics and phases overall. Therefore, player damage will now work as it did previously where you can attack the player if they do not control a card in an opposing lane and your card declares an attack.

In addition to this, icons are retaining their player damage effect from the damage phase. If an icon is in the back row and the opposing back row zone has no card in it, you may activate the icon to deal damage to the player. This counts as an attack and is the only time an icon can declare an attack.

### REWORKED: Spells

In the previous patch, I made it so you could play spell cards on the board. This mechanic is being refined and pushed further.

Spells can now ONLY be played on the board. You must place a spell in an open zone on the board, this is considered "summoning" the spell. While a spell is on the board, it acts as any other card on the board does. Any non-trigger effects passively apply, and they can be activated to use skills and targetted by effects that target cards. While on the board, spell cards are considered 0 force and can never gain force (unless a card effect specified otherwise).

A new trigger is being added called "cast". All spells inately are assumed to have a cast trigger that does nothing if one isn't explicity declared. When you "cast" a spell, it is sent from the grid to the discard. This does not count as the spell "perishing". If a card has an explicit "cast" trigger, its effect applies when the cast is declared. Unless specified otherwise, all casting can be done at instant speed. The instant keyword now only references the speed at which a card can be "summoned" or its skills can be used. I will be more specific below for the keywords that may become unclear due to this change.

Currently, this trigger is only on spell cards but I may add it to units/icons in a future set. If a unit/icon were to have the "cast" trigger, the same rules apply for them too except they are assumed to not have a cast trigger unlike spells. For set 1, this will not happen.

Note: cards with the effect keyword can only be attached from the hand. If you summon an effect spell you cannot attach it by casting it. Attaching a card is not considered summoning or casting it is a separate mechanic. However with this change, you can attach effects to spell cards if you want.

### Keywords

Instant

- Can be summoned to the board at any point in time or use skills at any point in time.

Effect

- Can be attached from the hand to a card you control.

### Triggers

Victor

- Is now overwritten by breach triggers when attacking a player (this was intended already but not made clear)
- Now also triggers when any instance of damage happens to the card and it survives.

### Draft

I've decided to make a small change to draft: instead of passing around the remaininng 2 cards after choosing 3, instead discard the cards. They can no longer be drafted.
