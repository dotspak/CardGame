# Pecto 2.0 Update

## Big Changes

- Free attacking from cards has been removed entirely.
- LVL has been removed entirely. All cards are free to play, with rituals used more often as card-specific costs.
- Cards are now either front-row cards or back-row cards, and can only be played in their respective row.

## Attack Phase

After your first main phase, you enter your Attack Phase.

When you enter your Attack Phase, each active front-row card you control must attack.

Attacking does not cause a card to become inactive.

For each attacking card, declare whether it is attacking the opponent or the opposing back-row zone in its column.

You may only attack a back-row zone if there is a card there.

After all attacks are declared, the defender may block attacks with their active front-row cards.

Each front-row card can only block the opposing attacking card in its column. Back-row cards cannot block.

If an attack is blocked, the attacking card and blocking card deal combat damage to each other.

If an attack is not blocked, it deals combat damage to its declared target:

- If the opponent was attacked, they take the damage.
- If the back row was attacked, the back-row card takes the damage.

Back-row cards do not deal combat damage.

Players cannot activate responses during the Attack Phase.

After the Attack Phase, the active player gets a second main phase.
