# Future Mechanics

## System Mechanics

Level X cards:

- Cards that can always be set from the hand, but get better the later in the game you play them.
- When a LVL X card is set, its LVL becomes whatever your minimum LVL cap is (acts normal once on the board).
- These cards include X somewhere in their text. X is always equal to LVL X card's LVL.

Yellow Skills:

- Yellow Skills can only be activated when a card is inactive.
- Similar to Green Skills, these can be activated any number of times.

## Triggers

Attack Trigger:

- An effect that occurs when an attack is declared, cannot be responded to.
  - Attack is delcared by card with attack trigger
  - Attack trigger resolves at the same time as declaring the attack
  - Defending player gets their normal response after

Warden:

- An effect that occurs when a card is targetted for an attack.
- Ordering works as such:
  - Attack is declared against a card with a warden trigger
  - Warden trigger activates
  - Defending player gets their normal response after

## Keywords

Agile:

- A card with this keyword becomes active during you endstep and upkeep.
- Cards with this keyword can use skills on your turn and on the opponents turn, as well as attack and then use a skill on the opponent's turn.

Double:

- A card with double deals damage equal to 2x their force.

Pierce:

- A card with pierce deals excess damage to an opposing back row card in the same row as the front row card it dealt damage to.

Cycling Equivalent:

- You may discard this card from your hand to draw a card.

## Skills

Friend Call:

- Reveal the top 5 cards of your deck, add a card with the same type as this card to your hand from among them.

Death Link:

- Set a card from your discard with Death Link.
