# Pecto Formats

All formats are assumed to be 20 cards unless stated otherwise

---

## Constructed Formats

All constructed formats unless stated otherwise are singleton.

### Standard/Constructed

- A deck can contain any 20 cards from among the current season of cards.

### 4-Zone Pecto

- Same rules as standard, except the game only has the left and right column.

---

## Draft Formats

The standard for drafting is to reveal 4 cards between 2 players. Players then take turns choosing 2 cards to add to their deck, with the order alternating each round. Stop after both players have 24 cards, then remove 4 cards from the deck to create your final 20.

### Standard Draft

- Each player receives 2 seasonal packs of 12 cards from the current season of cards.
- Decks may contain any number of a named card.

Duplicate Rule

- From Hand:
  - You cannot set a car if you already control a non-token card with the same name.

- From Card Effects:
  - If a card would enter play under your control with the same name as a non-token card you already control, it is sent to the discard instead. This is NOT considered entering the discard or perishing.

You may use any packs for casual play so long as every player ends with 24 cards.

### Cube Draft

- Draft singleton 20 cards between any amount of players.
- Combine 1 copy of every card in the current season into 1 pile and draft cards from that pile.

## Products

### Set boxes

- Contains 1 copy of each card from the expansion at the lowest rarity.
- Intended for casual cube draft or an entry point for constructed.

### Set Packs

- Contains 8 cards per pack.
- Guaranteed 2 rares, 1 super-rare.
- Any of these 3 guaranteed spots can rarely be a divine-rare, highly rare versions of cards often with alternate art or special atributes to their art.
- Only contains cards from a specific expansion

### Seasonal Packs

- Contains 12 cards per pack.
- Same rarity system as booster packs.
- Contains cards from all expansions in the current season.
- Which expansion the indiviudal pack contains is listed on the front.
- These packs are intended to be used for draft play, but can also be used for collectors.
