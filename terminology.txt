--------------------------------------------------
Pyramids
--------------------------------------------------
Discard Pyramid:
Dismiss
	A card is instantly sent to the graveyard
Erase
	A card is instantly sent to the void
Perish
	A card perishes when they lose all of their force. 
Discard
	Either dismissed or perished

Play Pyramid:
Summon
	Paying Cost, Rituals
Play
	No cost, no rituals

--------------------------------------------------
Keywords
--------------------------------------------------
Flying
	If the opposing card is not also flying or defender, these cards 	cannot block each other in combat.
Bleed
	Player who receives damage from this card takes 2 hits.
Defender
	This unit may automatically move to an open space being attacked, and
	can block flying units. Defenders deal 0 damage if they choose to 	attack.
Agile
	This unit can move to any open space or swap with any other unit on 	your grid when they move.
Static
	This unit cannot move or swap with another unit.
Haste
	This unit can attack on the first turn they're put into play.
Rooted
	This card cannot be swapped with another card.
Fragile
	If this card survives combat, they perish. If this card has a shield 	counter, decrement it instead.

--------------------------------------------------
Counters
--------------------------------------------------
Shield
	This card ignores damage taken, and loses a shield counter instead.
	Aside from fragile, shield counters do not prevent a card from 	perishing.
Sick
	When a card has any amount of sick counters, they are considered 	infected. At the start of your turn, an infected card loses a sick 	counter. If their sick counters ever hits 0, the card perishes.
