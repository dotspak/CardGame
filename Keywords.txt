Positive Keywords
Flying		Cannot attack or block a non-flying creature
Agile		Can move to any open space on the grid
Energetic	Creature can move and attack in one turn
Bleed		Deals double damage to the player
Haste		Creature can immediately act
Instant		Can be used at any time
Immortal	Cannot take damage

Negative Keywords
Fragile 	Creature dies after attacking
Static		Creature cannot move
Sealed		Cannot deal damage

Counters
Shield		Ignore damage taken, then remove a counter
Sick		At the start of the turn, remove 1 counter. The card perishes when the sick counters hit 0
