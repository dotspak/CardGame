im currently working on a card game with the current parameters:
20 card deck
1 copy of each card
5 life, every hit deals 1 damage
Cycling deck
Discard Pile, Exile Pile
Units fight the unit directly in front of them, they cannot ignore the unit and directly attack the player. If there is no unit to defend, 
the unit attacks the player, and that player may choose to block with their off-site card. The currency in this 
game are souls, and you passively gain 3 per turn. 

All cards fall into one category for costs:
Standard: Card costs 0-5
X: Card costs x souls
null: Card can only be summoned through an alternate method
Cards can also have something called "rituals" which is effectively an extra cost that must be completed to play the card. For example, I have
a card called "The Moon" that requires you to sacrifice 2 units to play it.

Heres a break down of each card type:
Units:
Typically cost 1-3 souls to play, and are the primary card type of this game. Units have a few stats they can have:
Force: Represents the unit's life and damage. When a unit enters combat it deals damage equal to it's force, and loses force equal to the damage they take in return. By default, a unit only has 0-10 force, 
but they can have more with card effects. ou may have 3 units on the grid any given time.
Bonus: Deals extra damage on top of their force
Shields: Prevents an instance of damage for each shield

Keywords for Units are:
Flying, Haste, 
Defender - Cannot attack, but may jump to any open lane being attacked
Fragile - Perishes after combat regardless of force
Static - Cannot move or be swapped with another unit
Agile - Can move to any open lane/swap with any unit
Bleed - Deals 2 damage to the player
Reach - Opponent cannot block the card with their off-site

Off-Sites:
Typically cost 0-2 souls (most commonly 0 souls), off-sites are the main defensive card of the game. Like units,
off-sites also have force, but force only represents their health. Off-sites can do damage if they have bonus. An 
off-site only enters combat when a player chooses to block an incoming unit with them. You may have 1 off-site on
the grid at any given time.

Keywords for Off-sites:
Totem: Can be placed beside an off-site as an extra bonus effect + stat increase (only 1 totem at a time)
Stadium: Can not have any totems added to it
Organic: Deals damage like a unit, where force is also damage.

Spells:
No typical cost, can range from 0-5. Spells are designed to help bring you towards your deck's goal and have various effects

Keywords for Spells:
Instant: Can be played at any time
Effect: is equipped to a unit, and is not discarded until the unit perishes/exits the grid

Based on this information, please make a deck of 20 cards. You may make the deck in any way you wish, but try to keep
a consistent theme. I would like you to give a breakdown of each card's stats, effects, keywords, and an explanation of why you added
them to the deck. Also note that the keywords given are exclusive to the card type.
